## Overview

Multiple regression is the workhorse of quantitative analysis at CFA Level 2. Where simple regression gave you one independent variable, here you control for many simultaneously — isolating the marginal contribution of each factor to the dependent variable.

<Callout type="exam-tip">
The exam loves to test your ability to diagnose violations of classical assumptions: heteroskedasticity, serial correlation, and multicollinearity. Know the test names and the remedies cold.
</Callout>

## Core Framework

<FormulaBlock label="Multiple Regression Model">
Y = b₀ + b₁X₁ + b₂X₂ + … + bₖXₖ + ε

Where:
- Y = dependent variable
- b₀ = intercept
- b₁…bₖ = slope coefficients for each independent variable
- ε = error term (assumed normal, mean zero, constant variance)
</FormulaBlock>

## Overview

Corporate payout policy sits at the crossroads of corporate finance and equity valuation. At Level 2, you need to understand not just the mechanics of dividends and buybacks but the informational content they carry. Why does a company choose one payout method over another, and what does that choice signal?

<Callout type="exam-tip">
The exam frequently tests the tax and signaling implications of dividends versus share repurchases. Know the conditions under which each method is preferred and the impact on EPS and share price.
</Callout>
